STRATEGY MECHANICS
================================================================================

ENTRY SIGNAL:
1. Monitor RSI(14) continuously
2. When RSI > 70 (overbought), arm the strategy
3. Calculate 5-bar swing low
4. Wait for price to dip below swing low
5. Place SHORT limit order at: swing_low + (ATR × 0.8)
6. If limit fills within 20 bars, execute SHORT trade

ENTRY PRICE: Limit order price (0.8x ATR above swing low)
STOP LOSS:   Swing high from RSI signal bar (adaptive)
TAKE PROFIT: Entry price × (1 - 0.08) = 8% below entry
MAX HOLD:    500 bars (~5 days on 15m candles)

EXIT CONDITIONS (first hit):
- TP Hit:  Sell at take profit price (8% gain)
- SL Hit:  Stop loss triggered at swing high
- Timeout: Hold 500 bars then exit at market

POSITION SIZING: 5% of current equity per trade (compounding)
